## üì¶ Why Use Expo Go?

Traditional mobile development depends on emulators, which require high-performance machines and frequent updates to match evolving devices (e.g., iPhone 7 ‚Üí iPhone 16 Pro Max, various Android models).

**Expo Go** offers a lightweight, cost-effective alternative:

- Test apps directly on your physical device.
- Supports both **iOS** and **Android**.
- Eliminates the need for emulators or native builds during early development.

---

## üì≤ Installation Steps

1. **Visit the Expo Go homepage**  
   [https://expo.dev/go](https://expo.dev/go)

2. **Select the latest SDK version**  
   Choose the most recent stable release for compatibility.

3. **Install Expo Go on your device**

   - **Android**: [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=host.exp.exponent)
   - **iOS**: [Apple App Store](https://apps.apple.com/app/expo-go/id982107779)

4. **Launch the Expo Go app**

5. **Create an account or log in**
